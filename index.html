Research Assistant

Metroplex Overflight Noise Analysis Project

The MONA project, in collaboration with ASCENT, provides real-time objective data, analyses, and reports to stakeholders and policy makers working to mitigate the noise impacts of FAA NextGen procedures.

The system collects ADS-B air traffic data, predicts noise impacts using tools including AEDT, visualizes large-scale datasets, and uses sound-level monitors deployed around the Bay Area.
