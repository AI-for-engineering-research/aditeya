Summary of the build (from AI)

This chat was a full iterative design session for my portfolio website. I asked for a clean, fast, dark-themed portfolio for Aditeya Shukla with smooth animations, an about section, a projects section, and project pages for about, updates, and reflections.

We refined the homepage repeatedly: reducing the text, making the tone more professional, adding my headshot, simplifying the hero, creating a playful three-line status caption, adding hover emoji bursts for Arsenal and Porchfest, and removing experiments that did not work well, like the custom airplane cursor.

We also rebuilt the About section into a concise bio plus a horizontally scrollable timeline, removed unnecessary sections, fixed navigation labels, updated the contact email behavior, and created a detailed project page for Aircraft Design for Contrail Avoidance with climate-tech styling, workflow diagrams, project details, and figures from the assets folder.
